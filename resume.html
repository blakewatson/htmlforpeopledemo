COSMIC

COSMIC is a comprehensive space hardware management system I help build for NASA in my role with MRI Technologies in Houston, TX. It meticulously tracks parts from initial receipt to installed on the EMU (spacesuit). It tracks activites, inspections, and an assortment of related documents and reporting. Its purpose is to ensure that every part going to space is ready and in a condition to do it safely.
