Using HTML to communicate with my houseplants

April 20, 2024

My ferns are thriving ever since I started coding their daily care instructions.

Who knew HTML could double as plant therapy? Here's how I made my houseplants the happiest on the block:

  1. Morning Pep Talk: I kick off each day with a cheery message like, "Rise and shine, green buddies! Time to soak up some sunlight!" The plants seem to respond with extra vigor.
  2. Weekly Motivational Quotes: Every Monday, I throw in a quote to inspire growth. "Don't stop beleafing!" seems to be a favorite.
  3. Custom Care Instructions: I cater to each plant's needs. My fern gets reminders to stay hydrated, while my cactus enjoys chill vibes messages about taking it easy.

It's quirky, it's fun, and it actually works.
