How I taught my cat to make a website with HTML

April 12, 2024

After just three days, Whiskers can now create web pages better than I can!

It all started when Whiskers, my ever-curious cat, sat on my keyboard during a coding session. Instead of shooing him away, I decided to take advantage of his interest. Little did I know, Whiskers had a knack for coding!

By the end of day one, he was familiar with basic tags like <html>, <head>, and <body>. On day two, he was creating paragraphs and lists. By day three, he had moved on to creating stunning layouts with <article> and <section>.

"HTML is elementary my dear Watson."
— Whiskers, probably

He's now accepting freelance projects.
